Wear a damn bike helmet

"Wearing a bike helmet makes you take more risks"

~Actual human garbage


There is a pernicious and dangerous lie I continue to hear regarding bike safety: That wearing a helmet actually makes you more likely to take risks while biking. The rationale being that the feeling of security offered by 400 grams of styrofoam turns us into careless idiots. As if you strap a bike helmet on and suddenly think you're inpervious to cars and trucks.

It takes 20 seconds to google statistics on how many lives are saved by bike helmets. One could argue that it takes more work to stay uninformed on the matter. What really gets my panties in a bunch is when I hear somebody casually repeat this particular bit of fake news.

Don't be that guy.

Don't like bike helmets?

Don't wear one
Fine by me

Just don't go telling people bullshit because you're too lazy [or vain] to protect your dumb brain.
Bikers get murdered by cars every fucking day.
Helmets save lives.

People said the same thing about seatbelts...

In the fifties.

When people smoked asbestos cigarettes and popped pharmaceutical amphetamine like it was candy.

I know what I'm talking about.

I like drinking and I like biking. I'm good at both. I have crashed on snow, I've crashed on gravel, I've crashed at night and during the day, I have broken bones and gotten stitches.

I shudder to think about that type of violence occuring to my head, unmitigated by a helmet. It totally bums me out.

Collectively, we are all struggling to cope with the complexties of this stupid world. If you really think you're kicking so much ass you'd accept a handicap, go jump in a lake. Wear a helmet.

Safety is cheap

Smashing your brain across an intersection is expensive.

Save yourself some medical bills by avoiding getting maimed in public. Nobody wants to see what your thinky-bits look like smeared all over the asphalt. Brains are gross.

Since our healthcare system in this country sucks as it is, don't muck things up by making emergency personnel have to deal with an avoidable brain injury.

Wear a helmet while biking.
